Quick-Capture Tools
To keep you "out of the loop" while still adding content:

Email-to-Bookmark: Set up a Cloudflare Email Worker so you can forward an email to add@d11.me. The Worker parses the link and adds it to Lumin automatically.

Mobile Share Extension: Use a simple iOS Shortcut or Android Intent that hits your /api/add endpoint so you can save links directly from your mobile browser.
