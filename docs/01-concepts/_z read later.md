# The "Read Later" Mode (Reader View Sync)

Since you’re already planning to scrape and clean up articles with Readability.js, give yourself a dedicated view for distraction-free reading.

The Feature: A "Reading List" tab that shows only the articles you haven't finished yet.

The Perk: Save the scroll position in D1 so you can start an article on your Mac at home and finish it on your iPhone later.
